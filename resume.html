Resume

In 2022 I graduated from the University of St Thomas with a degree in English with a focus in creative writing, and in 2023 I started at St Catherine University.

I have worked at the Minneapolis Central library, first as a volunteer working with the city pages collection, where I rehoused photographs with better organization, documented all the photographers and image content, researched image content to further organize and sort, and discarded unnecessary images in the collection. I have learned how traditional European paper was made, and how books, both modern and antique, can be cleaned and repaired to reenter circulation
